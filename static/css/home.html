Frequent Questions

1. Do mobile phone repairing shops provide screen replacement services?

Yes, from screen replacement to hardware issues, phone repair shops provide all mobile repair services. You can ask about the screen replacement service beforehand.

2. Is there a warranty on mobile phone repairs?

Yes, there is a warrenty on mobile phone repairs according to the different mobile products.

3. What quality of parts do repairing shops use in mobile phone repair and services near me?

Most reliable shops offer a limited-period warranty on mobile phone repairs. It is advised to know about the warranty terms and conditions beforehand.

4. Do I have to back up my data before availing mobile phone repairing service ?

Mobile phone repairing shops always use genuine parts. If original parts are not available, these service centres ensure to get OEM parts from the same factories that produce the parts, so you can be assured of the quality of service.

5. How much time do mobile phone repairing shops take for mobile phone repair and services ?

The time needed to fix a mobile phone issue varies depending on the problem at hand. Most minor mobile phone problems can be fixed within 30 to 40 minutes. If the problem is a major one, the technician may need to keep your handset for a day or two.
